Specialized Medical Departments

Our hospital features specialized departments with expert medical professionals and advanced technology

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive care for heart conditions, from diagnosis to treatment and rehabilitation.

  • Advanced cardiac imaging
  • Interventional procedures
  • Cardiac rehabilitation
  • Heart failure management
Learn More

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

  • Neurological diagnostics
  • Stroke treatment
  • Movement disorders
  • Epilepsy management
Learn More

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic department focuses on conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles.

  • Joint replacement
  • Sports medicine
  • Spine surgery
  • Trauma care
Learn More

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department provides specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents, addressing their unique healthcare needs.

  • Well-child visits
  • Childhood immunizations
  • Pediatric specialists
  • Child-friendly environment
Learn More

Dental Care

Our dental department offers comprehensive dental services for patients of all ages, from routine check-ups to complex procedures.

  • Preventive dentistry
  • Restorative treatments
  • Cosmetic procedures
  • Oral surgery
Learn More

Ophthalmology

Our ophthalmology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders and vision problems.

  • Comprehensive eye exams
  • Cataract surgery
  • Glaucoma treatment
  • LASIK procedures
